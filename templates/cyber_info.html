Cyber Frauds & Awareness

Police Inspector Impersonation Scam

Scammers pretend to be police officers, demanding money under false charges.

Action: Hang up and contact your local police helpline.

Video Call Cloning Scam

Fraudsters clone familiar faces to trick users on video calls.

Action: Verify identity by direct call.

Voice Cloning Fraud

AI-generated voices ask for money pretending to be relatives.

Action: Cross-check identity before sending money.

SMS & Loan Fraud

Messages claim you won a lottery or offer instant loans.

Dating App Scam

Fake profiles lure victims for emotional or financial exploitation.

Action: Never share private details or send money online.

Job / Talent Fraud

Scammers post fake job ads, asking for registration or interview fees.

Action: Verify company before payment.

Banking & OTP Fraud

Fake banking sites request OTP or credentials.

Action: Contact your bank directly.

Credit Card Fraud

Criminals misuse stolen card details for transactions.

Action: Monitor statements and block card immediately.

Cyber Helpline: 1930
Report: https://cybercrime.gov.in

Immediate Actions

  • Block your cards immediately.
  • Change passwords of banking & social media accounts.
  • Save scam numbers, messages, and screenshots.
  • Report the incident to your bank and/or telecom provider.
  • File a report with local cybercrime authorities if necessary.
